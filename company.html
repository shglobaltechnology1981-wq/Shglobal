About SH Global Technology

SH Global Technology is a trusted supplier of Industrial Sewing Machines, Genuine Spare Parts, Sewing Machine Attachments and Technical Services for the Ready-Made Garments (RMG), Textile and Apparel Industries throughout Bangladesh.

We provide sales, installation, maintenance, repair, machine setup, spare parts supply and technical support for all leading industrial sewing machine brands.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality Industrial Sewing Machines, Genuine Spare Parts, and reliable technical support that help garment factories improve productivity, quality, and efficiency.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading Industrial Sewing Machine solution providers in Bangladesh through quality products, excellent service, and long-term customer relationships.
